Roadmap

Where We Are, Where We're Headed

You're using Elikonas during our founding member alpha — which means you're seeing a real, working platform, not a finished one. We'd rather be upfront about that than pretend otherwise.

Below is what's live today, what we're building next, and what's further out. We'll keep this page current as things ship. And if you want a say in what we build next, tell us — the survey link at the bottom takes two minutes and directly shapes our priorities.

Available now

Learning record

Build your portable, owned record of ed-units — the credentials, courses, and skills you've built, all in one place that belongs to you.

AI guide, Eli

Talk through your goals and interests with Eli, your AI-powered guide, and get a pathway built around where you actually want to go.

Musings community

Share what you're learning and see what others are working on. This is where the Elikonas community actually happens.

Groups

Join or start a group around a shared topic or interest, and keep the conversation going beyond a single post.

Tidings (messaging)

Message other learners directly to connect, ask questions, or trade notes on a shared path.
