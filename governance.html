How We Govern This Website

Elikonas exists to give every learner a record they can trust — one that belongs to the user, for as long as they want it. We think the way we govern this website should reflect that same value: clear, honest, and easy to find.

Below are the agreements and policies that explain how we collect and protect your information, how our community works, and how we use AI. We'll keep this page updated whenever something changes, and we'll always let you know — right here, and in the app.
